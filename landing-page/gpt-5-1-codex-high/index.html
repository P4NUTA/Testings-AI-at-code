Is it another generic chatbot?

No. It is a workflow bot trained on your tone, connected to your channels, and judged by the conversations it keeps alive.

Slack, Discord, Circle, and email. The brain is hosted, the surface feels native. We log every action for auditability.

Content filters, hard guardrails, and an approvals queue for sensitive actions. You control thresholds and fallback scripts.

How much effort to maintain?

Weekly: review one digest and two suggested prompts. Everything else is automatic. Think of it as a colleague, not a tool.
