FAQ

Is this another chatbot?

Nope. It operates inside your stack, pulling signals from Slack, Discord, Circle, or Geneva, and only speaks when it has context.

Does it replace moderators?

Think of it as your ghostwriter + strategist. Humans stay in the loop; Campfire maintains rhythm.

What about privacy?

All data stays siloed. Models run in your dedicated enclave with full audit logs and off-switches.
